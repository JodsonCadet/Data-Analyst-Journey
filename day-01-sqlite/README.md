# 📊 Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
In this project, I analyzed sales data to understand product performance and revenue generation.

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Product name
- Price
- Quantity sold

## 🎯 Business Questions
- What is the total revenue? ($7,300)
- Which product generates the most revenue? **Phone ($2,500)
- Which products have high sales performance? **High Performing Products:** Phone, Laptop, and Monitor generated the highest revenue.

## 🛠 SQL Skills Used
- SELECT
- WHERE
- ORDER BY
- Calculations (price * quantity)

## 📈 Key Insights
- The **Phone generates the highest revenue**, making it the top-performing product in the dataset.
- Revenue is primarily driven by a combination of **price and quantity sold**, showing that both factors are important for business performance.
- Higher-priced products like the **Laptop and Monitor contribute strong revenue**, even with lower quantities sold.
- This analysis shows that focusing on high-performing products can help improve overall sales
