# 📊 Workforce Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

In this project, I analyzed employee workforce data to understand salary trends, department distribution, and employee tenure.

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

- Employee name
- Department
- Salary
- Years at company

## 🎯 Business Questions

- How many employees are in each department?
- Which department has the highest total salary?
- Which department has the highest average salary?
- Which employees earn more than $70,000?

## ✅ Answers to Business Questions

- Each department has 2 employees.

- The IT department has the highest total salary at $185,000.

- The Sales department follows with $155,000, then Marketing ($142,000), and HR ($125,000).

- IT also has the highest-paid employees overall.

## 🛠 SQL Skills Used

- SELECT
- WHERE
- ORDER BY
- GROUP BY
- COUNT
- SUM
- AVG

## 📈 Key Insights

- The IT department is the most expensive department, with the highest total salary.

- All departments have equal employee distribution, but salary allocation differs significantly.

- Higher-paying departments like IT may require specialized skills, which increases overall cost.

- Workforce analysis like this helps companies manage budgets and understand compensation trends.
