# 📊 Workforce Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
In this project, I analyzed employee workforce data to understand salary trends, department distribution, and employee tenure.

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Employee name
- Department
- Salary
- Years at company

## 🎯 Business Questions
- How many employees are in each department?
- Which department has the highest total salary?
- Which department has the highest average salary?
- Which employees earn more than $70,000?

## ✅ Answers to Business Questions
- The company has employees across HR, Sales, IT, and Marketing.
- IT has the highest total salary.
- IT also has the highest average salary.
- Several employees in Sales and IT earn more than $70,000.

## 🛠 SQL Skills Used
- SELECT
- WHERE
- ORDER BY
- GROUP BY
- COUNT
- SUM
- AVG

## 📈 Key Insights
- IT is the highest-paying department in the dataset.
- Sales and IT contain the highest-earning employees.
- Workforce analysis can help HR and leadership understand compensation trends by department.
